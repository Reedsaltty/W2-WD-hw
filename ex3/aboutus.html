About 616 GAMES

Hi, I'm Rafael Braza, the creator behind 616 Games. My biggest passion? That gritty, glorious era of '80s and '90s trash horror — the golden age of VHS tapes. I’m not just here to make horror games . I’m here to create unique, terrifying experiences that stick with you long after the screen fades to black. Right now, I’m focused on the "Tales Beyond the Tomb" series — a collection of short, standalone horror stories designed to thrill, unsettle, and surprise.
